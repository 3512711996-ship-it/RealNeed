# Evidence Policy

RealNeed separates search leads from verified evidence. A search snippet never
becomes strong or medium evidence by itself. A source must be accessible,
contain relevant extracted content, and show a concrete user complaint,
request, workaround, or repeated-friction scenario. Fewer than two independent
qualifying sources cannot produce a product opportunity.

`IDEA_SIGNAL_REPAIR` is intentionally different: it may explain evidence gaps
and propose validation work, but must not claim demand or payment intent is
verified.
