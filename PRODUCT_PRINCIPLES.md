# RealNeed Product Principles

1. RealNeed judges product opportunities; it does not manufacture startup ideas.
2. Technical outcome and market verdict are recorded separately.
3. Models cannot invent URLs, posts, titles, quotes, or source IDs.
4. Sources come only from a configured user search provider, verified user URL,
   user-pasted content, or explicitly labeled manual import.
5. Search leads are not evidence. Inaccessible, blocked, timed-out, or
   unverified sources cannot become strong or medium evidence.
6. Fewer than two independent qualifying sources cannot produce an opportunity.
7. Search, extraction, verification, and model failures are fail-closed; they
   are never reported as “no demand” and never use mock or template fallbacks.
8. Pasted text is labeled as user-provided and externally unverified.
9. Every opportunity must cite valid evidence, be beginner-feasible, fit a
   1-3 day MVP, include a first validation action, and state risks/stop rules.
10. Low confidence cannot be presented as a precise score or earnings promise.
11. Domestic monetization is a hypothesis to validate, not a promise.
12. All reports are free. Users provide their own search and generation API
    credentials; failure pauses the job rather than switching to a platform key.
13. Deep Dive links are hashed at rest, private, expiring, revocable, and can
    be regenerated or deleted by the report owner.
14. Historical payment records are read-only and never control a new report.
15. Donations and contact options are voluntary and never change access,
    report quality, quota, priority, or support eligibility.
16. API keys, passwords, recovery tokens, source bodies, and private links must
    not be logged, displayed to administrators, or committed to source control.
